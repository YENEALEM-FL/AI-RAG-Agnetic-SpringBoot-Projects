Elara’s entrance always paused the hallway chatter. Her striking beauty was a constant, but she consistently ignored the endless stream of compliments. For her, school was not a social scene; it was a launchpad for her future. In the third-floor library, far from the noise, she found true peace and focus. One afternoon, mapping a distant comet, the intense concentration in her eyes revealed her true self. She quietly sought knowledge, not popularity, ready to leave the Earth behind someday.
